About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on preparing professionals for cyber, digital and technology risk roles. By combining academic collaboration with real-world industry projects, IDR helps organisations and individuals manage complex digital risks.

Our Service Pillars

Academy

Training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals to develop expertise in digital and cyber risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new risk frameworks, AI governance models and future cyber risk solutions.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement standards like ISO 27001, NIST and NIS2 frameworks.
